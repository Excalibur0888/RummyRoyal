Online rummy has undoubtedly earned its place as India's favourite card game, and its vast array of variants is a significant contributing factor to its enduring appeal. With an abundance of options available, the rummy games ensure endless entertainment and never fails to captivate players. Once you have mastered a particular version, the allure of exploring new horizons beckons, urging you to delve into other thrilling rummy games. But why exactly are there so many rummy games? This ancient card game has traversed various countries throughout history, adopting diverse forms and adapting to the cultural nuances of each region it graced. Today, in the era of the internet, these numerous rummy games are readily accessible to you. All it takes is a simple download of the rummy app, and you can embark on an exhilarating journey of rummy gaming.

Here are some of the most popular rummy variants cherished by players in India on RummyRoyal:

Points Rummy

In Points Rummy, players engage in a rummy game where points are assigned to each card. Before the game, players agree on a rupee value per point. The goal is to declare with zero points, while the loser accumulates points based on the remaining cards in their hand. The winner receives cash based on the opponents' total points.

Deals Rummy

Deals Rummy is another captivating variant where a predetermined number of cards are dealt to each player at the beginning of the game. In online Deals Rummy, players receive a fixed number of chips to play with. At the end of each round, the winner acquires chips from the losing player based on the scores. The player with the highest chip count at the end of the game emerges as the winner.

Pool Rummy

Pool Rummy, a widely available variant in online rummy platforms, follows round-based gameplay. The game continues until a player accumulates over 101 or 201 points, depending on the chosen format. In each round, the winner earns zero points, making the game longer compared to other versions. The player with the fewest accumulated points at the end of the game emerges as the ultimate winner.
