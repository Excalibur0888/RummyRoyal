Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions below

How do I withdraw my winnings?

You can withdraw your winnings by going to the Wallet section in your account, selecting Withdraw, and following the instructions. Withdrawals are typically processed within 24-48 hours after verification.

What documents do I need for KYC verification?

For KYC verification, you'll need to provide a government-issued ID (such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, or Passport), proof of address (such as utility bill or bank statement), and a recent photograph. All documents should be clear and valid.

Is it legal to play rummy online in India?

Yes, playing rummy online is legal in most parts of India as it is considered a game of skill. However, players from Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Assam, Nagaland, and Sikkim are not allowed to play online rummy for prizes due to state regulations.

How can I download the RummyRoyal app?

You can download the RummyRoyal app from our website or directly from the Google Play Store for Android devices and the App Store for iOS devices. Visit our Rummy App page for more information.
